[Verse 2, Narrative Rap, Funky Groove, Confident, Electric Riff, Compression]

¡De la guarida de las ratas, hipnotizadas,
siguen al héroe con fe desenfrenada!

os guiaré al río con mi épica balada.

[Guitar Solo, Instrumental, Fast Arpeggios, Heroic, Lead Guitar, Overdrive]

[Bridge, Low Spoken, Slow Pulse, Menacing, Clean Bass, Dark Reverb]

hasta que el agua fría los reclina…

[Final Chorus, Choir Vocals, Double-time, Victorious, Power Chords, Massive Reverb]
